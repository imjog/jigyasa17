JIGYASA'17

11 - 12 November, 2017

Celebrating Physics at IIT BHU

ABOUT

The Annual Physics convention,JIGYASA is to be the meeting point of curious minds gathered in a common pursuit of knowledge. Following the legacy, JIGYASA'17 aims to provide a platform and share ideas and enlighten the participant about modern vistas of thought and technology. Along with two days of guest lectures by eminent physicists, we also bring up a bunch of exciting competitions like debate , quiz and much more. All one need is to -- STAY CURIOUS..

PREVIOUS GUEST LECTURES

Prof. Ashoke Sen

Padma Shri, Padma Bhushan, Dirac Medal awardee and a member of Royal Society nominated by Stephen Hawking himself, Ashoke Sen is a theoretical physicist and distinguished professor at HRI, Allahabad. His main area of research is String Theory.

Dr. J.N. Goswami

Dr. J.N. Goswami was a lead in Chandrayaan-1 mission. He received the INSA medal for Young Scientist(1978), the NASA Public Service Group Achievement Award(1986) and also the S.S. Bhatnagar award(1994).

Prof. Ajoy Ghatak

Prof. Ajoy Ghatak is a famous Indian physicist, an awardee of the S.S. Bhatnagar Prize for Science and Technology in Physical Sciences. He has written over 170 research papers and over 20 books ranging from Quantum Physics to Special Relativity and Fiber Optics in which he excels.
